Our Story

We built CareCliQ because admin shouldn't get in the way of care.

CareCliQ was founded in South Australia by a team who saw firsthand how much time NDIS support workers and coordinators lose to paperwork — especially workers whose first language isn't English. We built the tool we wished existed.

Every feature in CareCliQ was shaped by real conversations with support workers, coordinators, and business owners across the sector. This isn't a tech product designed in a vacuum — it's built for the people doing the work.

What CareCliQ Does

Everything your team needs to stay compliant.

Purpose-built tools that save real time — for workers writing notes and coordinators managing teams.

Write in any language. Comply in English.

Workers speak or type in Hindi, Tagalog, Nepali, Arabic, Swahili, Mandarin, or 7 other languages. CareCliQ reformats their words into a fully compliant NDIS progress note in under 30 seconds — automatically, in one step.

10+ Languages

Know your team's compliance score in real time.

Every note is checked against 12 NDIS documentation rules before it can be saved — goals referenced, objective language, 48-hour deadline, restrictive practice detection. Workers see their own score. You see it across your whole team without reading a single note.

12 NDIS Rules

Every credential. Every expiry. One place.

Workers store their NDIS Worker Screening, First Aid, Police Check, and CPR certificates in their CareCliQ credential wallet. You see expiry dates across your whole team and get alerts 30 days before anything runs out — before the Commission asks.

How CareCliQ works

01

Workers log in on any device

CareCliQ works on phones, tablets, and desktops — in the home, on the road, or in the office. No installation needed.

02

Speak or type in any language

Workers record their session notes in their first language — voice or text. CareCliQ handles the rest.

03

A compliant note is generated instantly

The note is translated, reformatted, and checked against 12 NDIS rules — all before the worker hits save.

04

Coordinators see the whole picture

Every compliance score, every expiry date, every flag — visible across your entire team in one dashboard. No chasing. No surprises.

Role-Based Access

Two roles. One platform. Everyone sees exactly what they need.

Support Coordinator

Full organisational visibility

  • Real-time compliance score across all workers
  • View every worker's session notes and flags
  • RP (restrictive practice) alerts — team-wide, immediate
  • Credential expiry overview for the whole team
  • One-click audit pack export for any participant
  • Weekly compliance digest emailed every Monday

Support Worker

Their sessions. Their score. Nothing else.

  • Their own compliance score and rule breakdown
  • Their sessions and participants only
  • Voice-to-text notes in any language
  • Suggestions to fix compliance issues automatically
  • Their own credential wallet
  • Session history summary before each shift

Workers cannot see other workers' data, invoices, or organisation settings. Data separation is enforced at the database level.
